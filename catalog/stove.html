Горелка - Trangia 25-2ha

Краткое описание товара:

Горелка Trangia 25-2ha(150252) Hardanodized ultralight aluminium. Kettle 0.9 litre. Weight 1025 g.

Подробные характеристики:

All camping stoves consist of two saucepans, 1.75 and 1.5 liters, a frypan Ø220 mm, windshields (upper and lower), a spirit burner, a pan grip and a strap. All camping stoves are available with a gas burner instead of the spirit burner. Dimensions (packed together): Ø220 mm x H105 mm.
